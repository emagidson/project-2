The Linus Foundation raises and donates tens of thousands of dollars to Local St. Louis charities and organizations annually. Linus puts on galas and other events where the admission fees go towards these causes four times a year. Coupled with donations, the money raised makes a real difference in the greater St. Louis community. The focus of these donations are to support organizations that help children in need.
In the fall of 2014, the St. Louis chapter was able to donate almost $15,000 to the St. Louis Crisis Nursery. The Crisis Nursery is an organization dedicated to ending child neglect and abuse. They provide emergency interventions and support for families of victims. Their five main tactics for providing aid are as follows: Short-term care for young children in a safe and nurturing environment Helping families resolve crisis Offering resources for empowerment, ongoing support and parent education. Community outreach and awareness Advocacy for children and families The Crisis Nursery, along with organizations that have recieved donations in the past, are all worthwhile causes that we hope to continue to support for many years to come.
