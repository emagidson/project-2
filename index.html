St. Louis Linus Foundation

The Linus Foundation is a community of young leaders dedicated to raising financial and volunteer SUPPORT for children’s charities in our local communities.
By growing and utilizing our extensive network of collegiate and young business leaders across the country, Linus orchestrates high-quality benefits and innovative PROGRAMS that result in an outpouring of support for local charities in every community with a Linus Branch.
To cultivate the next generation of non-profit and public leadership by providing talented young men and women an opportunity to lead and operate their own operation. In doing so, we provide lasting support for local children’s education and enrichment PROGRAMS and organizations.
